STAKE THE STIGMA

Empowering femininity

Destigmatizing step by step

It is time for women to have a place where they are comfortable to talk about their needs surrounding stigmatized topics and form a supporting community. We are developing an app that will create a safe space for women to talk about the sensitive topics that surround us, mainly regarding menstruation, contraception and abortion

There is a need for a more secured and open social community platform for women to share and support each other's health and wellbeing
